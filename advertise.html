Getting started advertising on Tōku

Disclaimer; Only verified users can create advertisments on Tōku

Using the advertising tool verified users can create custom announcements that will appear on the whats happening feed for a duration of time. Announcements can include Tōku chats, Google forms, Youtube links, and other websites (May take longer to verify). You can also choose an icon to show next to your announcement. To make an announcement please email the following info, your username and @, announcement title and description, website link (Optional).
