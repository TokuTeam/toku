Getting started advertising on Tōku

Disclaimer; Only verified users can create shouts on Tōku

Using the advertising tool verified users can create custom announcements (shouts) that will appear on the whats
happening feed for a duration of time. Announcements can include links to Tōku chats, Google forms, Youtube videos
and channels, and other websites (May take longer to verify). You can also choose an icon to show next to your
announcement. To make an announcement please email the following info, your username and @, announcement title
and description, website link (Optional), and title icon (Pick one from below).
