Getting started creating a Tōku discussion

Disclaimer; Only verified users can create discussions on Tōku

Creating a discussion on Tōku can help you organise your group or club, and getting started is easy! To start please
gather the following info.
  • Discussion name/short description.

  • Your username/@

  • 100 by 100 pixels icon.

Once you have the required info please email it to venomdevelop@gmail.com
