Getting started creating a Tōku chat

Disclaimer; Only verified users can create chats on Tōku

Email venomdevelop@gmail.com or mckinneyqu@wsdstudent.net the name of your chat/the description, as well as providing your username and @. Create a 100 by 100 pixels icon for your chat, this will be displayed on the Tōku home page. Make sure the icon is original and it clearly displays the topic of your chat. Once you have provided the info we will try to add your chat as soon as posible.
