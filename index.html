Whats UGD?
UGD (User Generated Discussions) Allows verified users to create their own chat. Verified users are people and friends
that we know and can trust. This means most chats are PG. (You may see some bad words depending on the chat) But
inappropriate images will not be tolerated as they are a bannable offence in all chats.
