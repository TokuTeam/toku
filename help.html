Frequently asked Questions

I was banned from a discussion what can I do?
We only unban users from Tōku discussions (For example Anything and Everything). If you get banned from a user
generated discussion it is up to the creator to unban you or not. Your best option would be contacting them yourself. In
the case that you are banned from a Tōku discussion please email venomdevelop@gmail.com.

How do I create a discussion?
Navagate to the Tōku home page then click the create button to learn how to create a chat.

What are featured channels?
The featured channels tab is used to promote people who feature Tōku on their YouTube channel. Unfortunately there
are no special perks or hidden secrets as it's just for fun, but if your featuring Tōku on your channel and would like to be
on the featured channels tab please email us a link to your channel.

What should I do if I see something suspicious?
We try our best make sure our website safe for everybody, but no moderation system is perfect. If you see something say
something!

How can I suggest a daily message?
On the Tōku home page click the header above the current daily message. You will be taken to a Google form where you
can suggest a daily message.
