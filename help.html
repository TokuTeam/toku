Frequently asked Questions

I was banned from a discussion what can I do?
We only unban users from Tōku discussions (For example Anything and Everything). If you get banned from a user
generated discussion it is up to the creator to unban you or not. Your best option would be contacting them yourself. In
the case that you are banned from a Tōku discussion please email venomdevelop@gmail.com.

How do I create a chat?
Navagate to the Tōku home page then click the blue chat bubble icon to learn how to create a chat.

What are Featured channels?
The featured channels area is a place where people who feature Tōku on their YouTube channel can have their channel
featured for everybody on the Tōku home page to see, unfortunately there are no special perks or hidden secrets as its
just for fun. If you are featuring Tōku on your channel and you want to be on the featured channels list please email us a
link to your channel.

What should I do if I see something suspicious?
We try our best make sure our website safe for everybody, but no moderation system is perfect. If you see something say
something!

How can I suggest a daily message?
On the Tōku home page click the green chat bubble icon next to the current daily message. You will be taken to a google
form where you can suggest a daily message.
