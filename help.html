Frequently asked Questions

I was banned from a discussion what can I do?
We can only unban users from Tōku managed discussions (Anything & Everything or The Underground). If you get
banned from a user generated discussion it is up to the creator to unban you or not. You can try contacting them yourself
but it is still their decision. In the case that you are banned from a Tōku managed discussion please email venomdevelop@gmail.com.

How do I create a chat?
Navagate to the Tōku home page then click the blue plus icon to learn how to create a chat.

What are Featured channels?
The featured channels area is a place where people who feature Tōku on their YouTube channel can have their channel
featured for everybody on the Tōku home page to see, unfortunately there are no special perks or hidden secrets as its
just for fun. If you are featuring Tōku on your channel and you want to be on the featured channels list please email us a
link to your channel.

What should I do if I see something sus?
We try our best make sure our website safe for everybody, but no moderation system is perfect. If you see something say
something!
