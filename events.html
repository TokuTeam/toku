How do I use the template?
- To use the template once you have downloaded it navigate to Piskel. On their website click "Create a Sprite". Then on your right there is a side bar with different icons. Click the one on the bottom that looks folder. Finaly choose to load a .piskel file. And your done! Now you can costomize it in anyway you like. When subminting your piskel make sure you change the pixel size to 32px before downloading.
